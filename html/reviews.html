Reviews

We have a lot of people who say good things about our company.

Respectable

I think this company is very respectable, keeping the best interest of all pets in mind. Knowing they are in great hands after coming from abuse is heartwarming. I love that they are willing to take in several types of animals, nurse them back to health and make sure they find a loving home. - Sue

Impressive

I got a lovely Rottweiler from this company named Zeus. I love that they take in dogs that may be considered "too aggressive" and give them another chance on life by letting people adopt them out. I was also able to adopt a water monitor named King and a parrot named OG. I love my animals and I love this company. - Julius

Fantastic

I would recommend this place! We have been searching everywhere for an older dog to keep company. We got the sweetest old lady. She is a 9-year-old beagle named Bella! - Kirstyn

Amazing Customer Service

The customer service was top notch, helpful. The animals were in great care. Sadly, we only had room for one in our family. - Andrew

Review by the Adams Family

We found our new family member Pugsly, at People to Animals. He is a nice addition to our family. After some training and TLC from all of us, Pugsly is learning to live with us. This organization truly gives the best care and helps these animals to heal before joining a new family. We are forever grateful. - Mrs. Adams

Over The Top

I give a 5 star; the adoption was quick. They even helped me with my new pet. They answered all my questions and person who was on the other end said that I could call back anytime I needed help with the animal. - Cynthia
