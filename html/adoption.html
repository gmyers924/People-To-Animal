Getting Involved

You should get involved today by donating, volunteering, and adopting.

Monetary Donations

These are accepted at any location at any time.

Supply Donations

We always accept donations of blankets, leashes, dog toys, beds, towels, brooms, rags, cleaning supplies, dog and cat food, scratching posts, cat toys, lizard or bird food, cages, and any supplies to help our animals to thrive.

Volunteer

Spend a day helping our staff, spending time with our animals, walking our dogs, socializing our cats, or caring for our animals.

Animals

We have many animals that are available in our facility and now it is time for you to adopt one. We are going to tell you the steps you need to know before getting a animal.

Dogs

Here are the items you need to have for a dog.

  • Dog bed and crate (if using)
  • Food and water bowls
  • Dog food
  • Collar, ID tag, and leash
  • Veterinary records

Dogs are very energic animals that needs lot of attention. So, make sure you bond with it and play fetch with them. Be prepared to take them on walks a lot. Make sure to be ready to buy a lot of dog food.

Cats

Here are the items you need to have for a cat.

  • litter box
  • cat litter
  • food and water bowels
  • food
  • scratching posts
  • safe and stimulating toys
  • cushy bed
  • brush for grooming
  • toothbrush
  • nail clippers

Lizards

Here are the items you need to have for a lizard.

  • Heating pad
  • Food
  • Calcium Powder
  • Aquarium/tank

Parrots

Here are the items you need to have for a parrot.

  • Cage lining (newspaper is best)
  • Medium sized cage
  • 3 or more toys for medium-sized parrots
  • Bird-safe cleaning supplies
  • Food and water dishes
  • Food for medium-sized parrots - Grain Products, Vegetables & Fruits, Dairy & Meat, and treats
  • Travel cage
  • Avian Veterinarian

Snakes

Here are the items you need to have for a snake.

  • Sturdy cage
  • Hiding places within the Cage
  • Adequate space
  • Good ventilation for his cage or tank
  • Clean, safe bedding and surroundings
  • Fresh water
  • Vitamin and mineral supplements
  • Veterinary care.
