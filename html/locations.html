History

Our organization began in 1965 on a farm outside of Harrisburg, PA.
It took a lot of work and a lot of people to convince but we did it. We knew that it would help bring the community together and help put an end to animal cruelty. We tried the first few years trying to convince people to stop with animal cruelty but there were some people who did not want to listen. They wanted to hunt animals down rather than keep them as a pet. So, we opened this company and changed the world.
We saw a need greater than our community and now have locations in PA, VA, and New York.

Our Future

If you are in the West Coast and have no ability to make it to one of our locations, well worry no more. We are currently working on a plan to get more locations and with your help today, it could be archivable.
We have plans on opening locations all over the United States and later Worldwide. We want to spread the word that animals should not suffer the pain.
