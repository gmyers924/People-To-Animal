People To Animals is a nonprofit organization where they tend to put an end to animal cruelty and to give the animals another chance at life.

Our Mission

You can be effective today by adopting an animal.
We aim to rescue animals and have them eventually, live with families. We try to find abused animals and give them a better home.
A few weeks in our facility, they will go to a foster family. After that we will set the animal for adoption so that it can go to a nice and loving family.

Foster Family

Pet fostering provides temporary care to shelter animals who need to live in a home environment prior to adoption. While pet fostering is ideal for some people, it is not for everyone. - petsforpatriots

Humane Society

We tend to end cruelty to animals and build a better community for animals to live in. Also, our belief is that animals are entitled to kind and respectful treatment.

Our No Kill Policy

We have a no kill policy where none of the animals are killed or injured while in our facility.
Once an animal is there for a long time, we donate it out for free. When that does not work out, we keep them.

Animal Abuse

If you suspect any animal abuse, you should contact the humane society at 717-555-5555. You can also try to contact us at 717-932-4242, and we will put an end to the abuse.
Another thing about us is that if we spot anyone abusing an animal, we will notify the authorities who will press charges on the abuser. Animals should not be abused.
